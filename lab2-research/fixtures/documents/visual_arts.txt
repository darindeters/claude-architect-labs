Title: AI's effect on visual arts: a 2025 survey
Source: https://example.org/visual-arts-ai-2025
Publication date: 2025-09-12

A 2025 industry survey of 1,200 professional visual artists found that 47%
report using generative AI tools at least weekly in their professional
workflow. Adoption is concentrated in concept art, reference image
generation, and rapid iteration during early-stage design.

The same survey reports that commission income for traditional digital
illustrators declined by approximately 18% year-over-year, with the steepest
declines in low-budget commercial work (book covers, stock illustration,
small-business branding). High-end editorial and gallery work was
substantially less affected.

A counter-trend has emerged: artists who incorporate generative AI as a
declared part of their workflow report stable or growing client bases,
particularly in studio environments where AI is one tool in a
human-directed pipeline.
