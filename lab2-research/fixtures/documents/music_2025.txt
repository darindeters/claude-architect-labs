Title: AI in music production — 2025 industry estimate
Source: https://example.org/music-revenue-2025
Publication date: 2025-10-22

A separate 2025 industry estimate places annual revenue from AI-assisted
music production tools at $680 million globally, citing rapid growth in
the assisted-mastering and stem-separation segments.

This figure differs notably from prior estimates published in 2024
(approximately $420 million). The two estimates reflect different
publication dates and different methodologies — the 2025 figure includes
revenue from in-DAW AI features that were not categorized as standalone
tools in the 2024 methodology.

Adoption among independent producers in 2025 is estimated at 54%, up from
31% in mid-2024.
