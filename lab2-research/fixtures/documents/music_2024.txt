Title: How music producers are using AI — 2024 mid-year report
Source: https://example.org/music-production-ai
Publication date: 2024-06-15

Among independent producers surveyed in mid-2024, generative AI tools were
used regularly by 31% of respondents, primarily for stem separation, drum
programming, and demo-stage composition. Adoption among major-label
production teams was lower, around 12%, citing rights and licensing
concerns.

Annual industry revenue from AI-assisted music production tools was
estimated at $420 million globally for 2024.
