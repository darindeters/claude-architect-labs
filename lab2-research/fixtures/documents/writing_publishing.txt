Title: AI tools in writing, journalism, and publishing
Source: https://example.org/writing-and-publishing-ai
Publication date: 2025-07-18

Newsroom adoption of AI assistance tools accelerated through 2024 and 2025,
with 62% of major publishers reporting integration of at least one AI tool
in their editorial workflow by mid-2025. Use cases concentrate in headline
generation, summarization, transcription, and fact-checking support.

Professional ghostwriting and editing services report mixed effects.
Demand for AI-augmented editorial services has grown, while demand for
purely human "rewrite" work has compressed by an estimated 12-15% over the
same period.

The disclosure debate remains unresolved across publishers, with no
industry-wide standard for crediting AI assistance in published work.
